Our Detailed Cleaning Checklist

KITCHEN

  • Clean and polish all cupboards and drawers both inside and out
  • Deep clean both inside and outside refrigerator and freezer
  • Deep clean and degrease inside of oven, polish outside parts, hob and grill
  • Wash and wipe down kitchen countertops
  • Remove mould and grease from wall tiles, wash down and polish
  • Clean windows from the inside and out, wipe down sills and ledges
  • Vacuum and mop hard floors

BEDROOM(S)

  • Wipe down doors, fittings, hinges & damp wiped and door tops
  • Wash mirrors/windows internally including sills and ledges
  • Dusting & Wipe clean walls
  • Clean inside and outside and on top of cupboards/wardrobes and furniture
  • Clean curtain rails and behind curtains
  • Clean sockets, plugs, light switches, fixtures and fittings
  • Wipe down skirting boards, vacuum and mop all hard floors

LIVING ROOMS

  • All carpeted areas vacuumed
  • All hard floors vacuumed & washed
  • Upholstery vacuumed & tidy
  • Clean & polish mirrors
  • All cobwebs removed from ceilings/ walls
  • Dusting & wipe clean walls
  • All doors fittings, hinges dusted & damp wiped
  • All radiators & skirting boards dusted and damp wiped

BATHROOM(S)

  • Clean and disinfect toilets from inside and out
  • Clean bath and removing limescale
  • Wash shower cabinets and de-scale screens from inside and out
  • Wash down and de-scale sink and shine taps
  • Clean extractor fan/ ventilation
  • Wash down tiles, remove mould and wipe tiles
  • Clean and polish mirrors and towel rails
  • Wipe cupboards, shelves, surfaces and polish steel
  • Vacuum and mop hard floors

RECEPTION ROOMS/STAIRWAYS

  • Wipe down stairways and railings
  • Clean behind and polish mirrors
  • All cobwebs removed from ceilings & walls
  • Clean behind, inside and on top of cupboards and furniture
  • Dust and polish all flat surfaces and carpets
  • Wipe down all skirting boards and wood works of dust

UTILITY/ LAUNDRY ROOMS

  • Clean & polish inside and outside of all cupboards/ drawers
  • Exterior of washing machine washed
  • Clean behind machine if accessible
  • Soap drawers & filters cleaned if accesible
  • Wipe down walls internally
  • Wipe down all woodworks & doors of damp/dust

Carpets & Upholstery - Vacuuming & soft furnishing included. If professional steam clean is required quote can be given on booking once pictures are given.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book your services?

You can book our services through our website, by telephone, or via WhatsApp. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2 hours during business hours and can often arrange same-day service for urgent matters.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all areas within the M25 and throughout Greater London, as well as select areas in Berkshire. Our strategically placed technicians ensure prompt response times across our service area.

Do you charge for parking and congestion charges?

No, all parking fees and congestion charges are included in our call-out fee.

How do you charge for materials?

Materials are charged separately from our labour rates. We provide transparent pricing for all materials needed, with itemised receipts provided. For larger jobs, we can provide a complete quote including materials in advance.

What are your payment terms?

We accept direct bank transfers and credit/debit card payments. Payment is required upon completion of works. For larger projects, we require a deposit before commencing work.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a minimum of 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid a cancellation fee. Emergency situations will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Do you offer commercial maintenance contracts?

Yes, we provide bespoke maintenance contracts for commercial properties, including scheduled maintenance, priority response, and preferential rates. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What insurance do you carry?

We maintain comprehensive public liability insurance up to £5 million. All our technicians and subcontractors are fully insured for complete peace of mind.
